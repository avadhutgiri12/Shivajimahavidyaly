About Our Institution

Empowering rural youth through excellence in education, character building, and social commitment since decades.

History and Heritage

Established with a vision to provide quality education to the rural masses, Shivaji College Udgir has a rich legacy of academic excellence and social commitment spanning several decades. Rooted in the principles of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, we strive to build a generation of scholars who are both technically proficient and ethically grounded.

Over the years, the institution has evolved from a small teaching center into a premier hub of higher learning, serving thousands of students from Udgir and surrounding rural areas of Maharashtra.
